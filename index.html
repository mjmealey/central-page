Rainbow Support

About

Rainbow Support is a newsletter created by Jake Mealey, an advocate for the LGBTQ+ community and a motivator for his fellow LGBTQ+ members.

The LGBTQ means the world to him as he also identifies as bisexual and asexual. The Rainbow Support newsletter contains images that seeks

to motivate LGBTQ members to stand up for what's right. The newsletter also contains quotes and history moments where the LGBTQ+ community won to show how capable we are

in the face of adversity. We are human and just because we are different from others that does not make us any less human.
